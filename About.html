Hi, I’m Desmond, an aspiring product designer.

I’m an industrial and product designer based in Vancouver, BC Canada, currently studying at SFU’s School of Interactive Arts & Technology. Shaped by a curiosity to dissect products and systems, I am always eager to discover how I can guide others towards more practical solutions and thinking. At the same time, I love imbuing moments of playfulness and delight in any product. I believe good design should always be at least a little bit fun for the creator to make, and for others to use.

Industrial Design

Spatial Design
